RAISE

Companies will be able to create a profile and start funding campaigns to raise equity capital from multiple investors.

INVEST

Accredited investors will be able to browse investment opportunities and easily participate in a funding round.

SYNDICATE

Lead investors can form a syndicate of investors for a deal and earn a “carry” fee from members in the syndicate.
